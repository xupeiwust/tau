# Cookie Policy

*Effective date: December 29, 2025*

This Cookie Policy explains how TauCAD Limited ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our Service. This policy should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy).

This Cookie Policy complies with the EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC as amended by 2009/136/EC), the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), and other applicable cookie laws.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Similar technologies like localStorage are also used to store information locally in your browser.

## Cookies We Use

We distinguish between two types of cookies:

- **Essential**: Strictly necessary for basic functionality. Cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics** (Optional): With your consent, used to understand how you use the Service.

### Essential Cookies

| Category | Purpose | Retention |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Theme** | Remember your preferred color theme and accent color | Persistent |
| **Layout** | Store panel sizes, sidebar states, and UI preferences | Persistent |
| **CAD Settings** | Remember CAD kernel, measurement units, and file preview settings | Persistent |
| **Chat Preferences** | Store AI model selection, web search preferences, and interface settings | Persistent |
| **View Settings** | Remember camera field of view, view configuration, and section view settings | Persistent |
| **Console Settings** | Store log level and display configuration | Persistent |
| **Converter Settings** | Remember output formats and download preferences | Persistent |
| **Cookie Consent** | Remember your cookie consent choice | 1 year |

These cookies are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to external services. "Persistent" cookies remain until you clear your browser data or reset preferences.

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we use PostHog to understand how users interact with our service.

**Retention:** 12 months from collection.
**Data Location:** United States.

**What PostHog collects:** Page views, navigation patterns, device and browser information, interaction patterns, feature usage statistics.

**What PostHog does NOT do:** Use third-party cookies, track you across websites, share data with advertising networks, sell your data.

For more information, see the [PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy).

## Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal. If your browser sends a GPC signal:

- Analytics cookies (PostHog) will not be loaded
- Your preference is respected immediately without displaying a consent prompt for analytics
- Essential cookies will still function as necessary for the Service

For more information about GPC, visit [globalprivacycontrol.org](https://globalprivacycontrol.org).

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit Tau, you will see a cookie consent banner where you can:

- **Accept**: Enable both essential and analytics cookies
- **Decline**: Enable only essential cookies (analytics disabled)
- **Manage**: Customize your choices

You can change your preferences at any time:

1. Click the Cookies button in the footer to reopen preferences
2. Visit [Cookie Settings](/legal/cookies#preferences)
3. Use your browser's cookie controls
4. Clear site data for tau.new to reset all preferences

When you withdraw consent for analytics, we stop collecting new data immediately.

## Browser Cookie Controls

Most web browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies through their settings. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.

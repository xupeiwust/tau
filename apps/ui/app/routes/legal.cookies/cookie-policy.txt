# Cookie Policy

*Effective date: December 23, 2025*

This Cookie Policy explains how TauCAD Limited ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our Service. This policy should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy), which provides more information about how we collect and process your personal data.

This Cookie Policy complies with the EU ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC as amended by 2009/136/EC), the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR), and other applicable cookie laws.

## What is a Cookie

Cookies are small text files which may include an anonymous unique identifier. They are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your device. Similar technologies like local storage are also used to store information locally in your browser.

## How Tau Uses Cookies

Tau uses cookies and similar technologies like local storage to maintain your preferences and hold certain information to improve your experience.

We distinguish between two different types of cookies:

- **Essential**: Cookies that are strictly necessary for basic website or app functionality. These cannot be disabled as the service would not function properly without them.
- **Analytics**: Cookies used to collect information about how you use the website. This data helps us improve the site and your experience. These are optional and require your consent.

## The Cookies We Set

### Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies to remember your preferences and settings. These include:

| Category | Purpose | Retention |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| **Theme** | Remember your preferred color theme and accent color. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **Layout** | Store panel sizes, sidebar states, and UI preferences so your workspace looks the same each time you visit. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **CAD Settings** | Remember your preferred CAD kernel, measurement units, and file preview settings. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **Chat Preferences** | Store your selected AI model, web search preferences, and interface settings. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **View Settings** | Remember your camera field of view, view configuration, and section view settings. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **Console Settings** | Store your preferred log level and display configuration. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **Converter Settings** | Remember your preferred output formats and download preferences. | Persistent (until cleared) |
| **Cookie Consent** | Remember your cookie consent choice so we don't ask you again. | 1 year |

These cookies are stored locally in your browser using localStorage and are not transmitted to any external services. "Persistent" cookies remain until you clear your browser data or reset your preferences within the application.

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

With your consent, we use PostHog to understand how users interact with our service. PostHog is an open-source product analytics platform that helps us improve Tau.

**Retention:** Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months from the date of collection.

**Data Location:** PostHog processes analytics data in the United States.

**What PostHog collects:**

- Page views and navigation patterns
- Device and browser information
- Interaction patterns (clicks, scrolls)
- Feature usage statistics

**What PostHog does NOT do:**

- Does not use third-party cookies
- Does not track you across different websites
- Does not share data with advertising networks
- Does not sell your data to third parties

You can opt out of analytics cookies at any time through our cookie preferences banner or by visiting your browser settings.

For more information, see the [PostHog Privacy Policy](https://posthog.com/privacy).

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

When you first visit Tau, you will see a cookie consent banner where you can:

- **Accept**: Enable both essential and analytics cookies
- **Decline**: Enable only essential cookies (analytics will be disabled)
- **Manage**: Open detailed preferences to customize your choices

### Changing Your Preferences

You can withdraw or modify your cookie consent at any time using any of these methods:

1. **Cookie Settings Button**: Click the Cookies button in the footer of the homepage to reopen the cookie preferences dialog
2. **Direct Link**: Visit [Cookie Settings](/legal/cookies#preferences) to manage your preferences
3. **Browser Settings**: Use your browser's built-in cookie controls to view, delete, or block cookies
4. **Clear Site Data**: In your browser's settings, you can clear all data for tau.new to reset all preferences

When you withdraw consent for analytics cookies, we will stop collecting new analytics data immediately. Previously collected data will be retained according to our retention policy but will not be supplemented with new data.

## How to Control Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

- View what cookies are stored
- Delete cookies individually or all at once
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies (note: this may affect site functionality)

Please refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions on managing cookies.

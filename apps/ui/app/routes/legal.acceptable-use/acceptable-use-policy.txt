# Acceptable Use Policy - TauCAD Limited

*Effective date: December 29, 2025*

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the TauCAD Limited software service (the "Service"). This AUP is incorporated by reference into our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms). By using our Service, you agree to comply with this AUP.

We may update this AUP from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated AUP.

## 2. General Conduct

You agree to use the Service only for lawful purposes and in accordance with our Terms of Service. You are responsible for your conduct and any content you create, upload, or share through the Service.

## 3. Prohibited Activities

You shall not use the Service to:

| Category | Prohibited Activities |
|----------|----------------------|
| **Illegal Activities** | Use the Service for illegal purposes; violate laws, regulations, or third-party rights; facilitate illegal activities |
| **Harmful Conduct** | Transmit viruses, malware, or harmful code; breach security measures; interfere with or disrupt the Service; overload or spam the Service |
| **Abuse & Harassment** | Harass, threaten, stalk, or intimidate users; engage in bullying; promote violence or discrimination based on protected characteristics |
| **IP Violations** | Infringe copyrights, trademarks, patents, or trade secrets; upload content you don't have rights to; remove proprietary notices |
| **Deceptive Practices** | Impersonate persons or entities; create fraudulent accounts; engage in phishing or social engineering |
| **Unauthorized Access** | Access unauthorized parts of the Service; collect information without authorization; use unauthorized bots or scrapers |
| **Service Abuse** | Exceed usage limits; circumvent restrictions; resell access without authorization; damage or impair the Service |

Pseudonymous accounts are permitted provided that required contact information (email, billing) is accurate.

## 4. Content Standards

User Content must not contain or promote:

| Category | Prohibited Content |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Offensive** | Defamatory, obscene, hateful, or inflammatory material; sexually explicit material; graphic violence; shocking or disgusting content |
| **Discriminatory** | Discrimination based on protected characteristics; hate speech; content that demeans individuals or groups |
| **Deceptive** | Misleading information; false statements; impersonation; content falsely appearing to originate from us |
| **Illegal** | Promotion of illegal activities; content breaching legal duties; IP infringement |
| **Harmful** | Threatening or abusive content; privacy violations; harassment |
| **Spam** | Unsolicited advertising; unauthorized promotional material; spam or phishing campaigns |

## 5. AI-Generated Content

When using our AI-assisted features, you are responsible for:

- Reviewing and verifying AI-generated output before use
- Ensuring AI-generated content complies with this AUP and applicable laws
- Not using AI features to generate prohibited content
- Not attempting to manipulate AI systems to produce harmful outputs

## 6. CAD-Specific Guidelines

When using our CAD features, you shall not:

- Create designs primarily intended for weapons or devices to cause physical harm, or items prohibited by law
- Upload or generate content infringing third-party design patents, trade secrets, or IP rights
- Reverse-engineer proprietary designs without authorization
- Share or export designs in violation of export control regulations (see Section 6.1)

### 6.1 Export Control Compliance

Certain CAD designs (aerospace, defense, encryption, dual-use items) may be subject to export control regulations including U.S. EAR, ITAR, and equivalent regulations in other jurisdictions.

You are solely responsible for:
- Determining whether your designs are subject to export restrictions
- Obtaining required export licenses before sharing controlled designs
- Complying with sanctions and embargo requirements

We reserve the right to restrict access from embargoed countries or regions as required by law.

## 7. Child Safety

We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and content that exploits or endangers children.

**Prohibited:** Uploading, sharing, creating, or distributing content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates child sexual abuse; sexualizes minors; or facilitates contact with minors for sexual purposes.

**Enforcement:** We will immediately remove such content, report to NCMEC and authorities, permanently terminate accounts, and cooperate with law enforcement.

Report violations to safety@tau.new.

## 8. Reporting & Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

Report content or behavior that violates this AUP to support@tau.new.

### Enforcement Actions

Violations may result in:
- Warning or notice
- Content removal or restriction
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent termination
- Reporting to law enforcement

### Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error, contact support@tau.new with:
- Description of the action taken
- Reason you believe it was in error
- Relevant context or evidence

We will respond within 10 business days. Decisions following appeal are final.

### No Monitoring Obligation

While we may monitor and enforce this AUP, we are not obligated to do so. Users are encouraged to report violations.
